About Casa de Arte


Casa de Arte means Home of Arts i Spanish. It is an online Art Gallery store created by Group 16. Works of many famous artists are sold here. The site provide many features along with easy payment methods for users. Top quality paint, colours etc are are also available here at reasonable prices. Casa de Arte is a user-friendly site and very easy to use. Our main objective is to spread the COLOURS OF JOY through art.
